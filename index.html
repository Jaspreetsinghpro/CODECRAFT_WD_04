About Me

Hello! I'm Jaspreet Singh, a passionate and results-driven Web Developer currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. I specialize in building dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly websites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. With a strong command over HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, I bring ideas to life through clean, efficient, and maintainable code. On the backend, I have experience working with Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, enabling me to build full-stack web applications from scratch. I enjoy the entire development process—from brainstorming ideas and designing intuitive user interfaces, to coding, testing, and deploying fully functional web applications. I'm particularly interested in modern UI/UX design, interactive front-end development, and solving real-world problems through code. In addition to my technical skills, I’m constantly exploring the latest trends in web development and always eager to learn something new. Outside of coding, I enjoy sketching, playing chess, and exploring AI-driven tech that can improve everyday digital experiences. My goal as a developer is not just to write code, but to create meaningful digital solutions that are impactful, scalable, and user-centric.
